Peace through Remembrance

Transliteration

śiveti mantram uccārya yaḥ smaret satataṁ naraḥ
sarva-pāpa-vinirmuktaḥ śāntiṁ yāti na saṁśayaḥ

One who constantly remembers Śiva by repeating the mantra "Śiva" becomes freed from inner disturbances and attains peace — without doubt.
